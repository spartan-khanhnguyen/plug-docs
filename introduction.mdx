# Welcome to PlugnPay

PlugnPay is a comprehensive payment and subscription management platform designed to simplify how developers integrate payment processing, subscription billing, and usage tracking into their applications.

## What is PlugnPay?

PlugnPay provides a unified API and SDK suite that handles:

- **Payment Processing**: Secure checkout sessions and payment handling
- **Subscription Management**: Automated billing cycles and subscription lifecycle management
- **Access Control**: Feature gating and usage-based access controls
- **Usage Tracking**: Real-time monitoring of feature usage and consumption
- **Webhook Management**: Complete webhook processing and event delivery - no webhook setup required
- **Analytics**: Comprehensive insights into customer behavior and revenue metrics
